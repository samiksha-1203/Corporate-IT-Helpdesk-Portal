Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check the status of my ticket?

All your tickets are listed in the "My Tickets" section with their current status clearly displayed.

What's the typical resolution time for tickets?

Resolution times vary by ticket type:

  • Critical issues: 2-4 hours
  • Hardware issues: 1-3 business days
  • Software issues: 1-2 business days
  • General inquiries: 1-5 business days
How can I update or add information to my ticket?

Please reply to the email notification you received when the ticket was created, or contact our support team directly with your ticket number. We'll update the ticket with any additional information you provide.
